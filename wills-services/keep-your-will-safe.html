Chelmsford Will Storage Solutions

Storing with a Solicitor

If you had a lawyer draw up your will, he or she will usually keep your will at no charge.

If they did not draft your will, they might provide storage for a fee.

🔗 Speak to trusted Will Writers Chelmsford for further storage assistance.

You may also apply to the Principal Probate Registry, as there is an official, secure location for the storage of wills.

Simply fill in a straightforward form and a storage certificate will be issued if you do for a one-off £20 fee.

Hang on to this certificate your executor will need it to get the will.

We can even supply secure postal services (tamper proof envelopes) to get your document delivered safely.

Storing Wills in Chelmsford at Home You might want to hold your will at home, but proceed with caution.

These include the risk of damage from fire and water, and the increased risk of it being lost or stolen.

If you bring the will home, store it in a safe but fireproof container and let your executor know where it is located.

Self Storage Chelmsford With a Storage Company You could also keep your will with a private storage company.

These firms come with different sets of services and protections.

Investigate how they safeguard documents, for example by using locked, fireproof vaults, and verify that they are insured.

Also know how your executor will go about retrieving the will, as there may be annual storage fees.
